# Rooted One-Page Website Wireframe Options

These options were generated from 3 separate design passes based on your current logo-led earthy brand direction.

## Option 1: Earthy Premium

Style direction:

- Refined and premium feel with natural tones and soft textures.
- Elegant serif-forward typography and generous whitespace.
- Best fit if you want a high-trust, established look.

Palette:

- `#f0efe8` (base cream)
- `#dddbc9` (warm neutral)
- `#1f261f` (deep forest text)
- `#4f5a4f` (muted text/borders)
- `#556b2f` (olive accent)
- `#8B7D5B` (premium CTA accent)

Type pairing:

- Headlines: Garamond/Georgia-style serif
- Body/UI: Source Sans Pro + Karla

Wireframe structure:

1. Sticky header with logo and simple nav
2. Hero split layout (headline + logo)
3. Three service cards (Plumbing, Earthworks, Landscaping)
4. Trust section with reasons and stats
5. Project highlights + testimonials split section
6. Contact form block
7. Footer with services/company/contact

Contact form fields:

- Full Name
- Email
- Phone
- Service Needed (dropdown)
- Suburb/Location
- Message
- Consent checkbox
- Submit button

Motion ideas:

- Staggered reveal in hero and cards
- Count-up stats in trust section
- Subtle lift/hover states on cards and buttons

Good for:

- Premium residential + commercial positioning
- Strong professionalism and trust cues

---

## Option 2: Bold Field Utility

Style direction:

- Strong, practical, and industrial-clean aesthetic.
- High-contrast sections and structured grid composition.
- Best fit if you want to emphasize reliability and capability.

Palette:

- `#1a2419` (primary dark)
- `#6b8e23` (utility green accent)
- `#a67c52` (earth warm accent)
- `#f5f3f0` (light background)
- `#e8e6e3` (neutral section background)
- `#d4d0ca` (divider/border)

Type pairing:

- Headlines/UI: IBM Plex Sans
- Body: Source Serif Pro

Wireframe structure:

1. Bold split hero with large heading and CTA
2. Services in clean 3-column utility cards
3. Dark trust band with reason bullets
4. Project/testimonial card grid or carousel
5. Two-column contact section (form + direct contact details)
6. Information-dense footer

Contact form fields:

- Name
- Phone
- Email
- Service Needed (dropdown)
- Suburb/Location
- Message
- Consent checkbox
- Submit button

Motion ideas:

- Light reveal-on-scroll for cards
- Input focus and button feedback transitions
- Minimal, crisp interactions over decorative effects

Good for:

- Trade-first branding
- Practical and confident service messaging

---

## Option 3: Natural Craft Studio

Style direction:

- Organic, human, and crafted visual language.
- Asymmetrical rhythm, editorial spacing, and tactile vibe.
- Best fit if you want a distinctive, design-led identity.

Palette:

- `#f0efe8` (off-white base)
- `#dddbc9` (dust neutral)
- `#1f261f` (forest text/anchors)
- `#4f5a4f` (sage secondary)
- `#556b2f` (moss CTA/accent)
- `#a9ba84` (light moss highlights)

Type pairing:

- Headings: Fraunces
- Body/UI: Manrope

Wireframe structure:

1. Full-height story-led hero with strong headline
2. Asymmetrical service cards (3 core services)
3. Trust reasons with offset visual blocks
4. Project highlights + client story cards
5. Centered contact section with strong form hierarchy
6. Dark, clean footer

Contact form fields:

- Name
- Phone
- Email
- Service Needed (dropdown)
- Location/Suburb
- Message
- Consent checkbox
- Submit button

Motion ideas:

- Gentle fade/slide on section entry
- Slight card lift and border color shifts
- Form focus rings and clear validation states

Good for:

- Brand differentiation
- Friendly but premium local-business feel

---

## Recommendation

If your goal is maximum trust + broad audience appeal, pick **Option 1 (Earthy Premium)**.

If your goal is strong trade credibility and practical confidence, pick **Option 2 (Bold Field Utility)**.

If your goal is a memorable, crafted brand feel, pick **Option 3 (Natural Craft Studio)**.
